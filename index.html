Nurturing Leaders of Tomorrow

Faizy Course

Comprehensive Islamic Scholarship

The Faizy course is a flagship programme designed to produce profoundly knowledgeable Islamic scholars and leaders who can guide the community effectively.

  • Curriculum: Deep focus on Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, Aqeedah, and Arabic Literature.
  • Career Path: Graduates become Khatibs, religious scholars, teachers, and community leaders.
  • Skill Development: Leadership, public speaking, academic research, and spiritual discipline.

Daras System

Traditional Learning Meets Modernity

The Daras System offers a robust, residential learning environment that carefully balances classical Arabic and Islamic studies alongside regular school education.

  • Structure: Study circles traditionally held inside mosques or specialised classrooms with close mentorship.
  • Outcome: Ensures students do not miss mainstream academics while absorbing deep Islamic values.
  • Advantage: Cultivates incredibly strong moral foundations and ethical perspectives in youth.

Quran Hifz System

Preserving the Divine Words

Our dedicated Quran Hifz programme guides students in fully memorising the Holy Quran under the supervision of highly qualified and experienced teachers.

  • Methodology: Systematic daily memorisation with stringent regular revision schedules.
  • Tajweed: Heavy emphasis on perfect pronunciation and rules of recitation.
  • Spiritual Growth: Instills immense discipline, focus, and a lifelong spiritual connection with the Quran.
